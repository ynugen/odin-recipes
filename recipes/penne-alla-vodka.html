Penne Alla Vodka

Description

A lovely tomato and cream based pasta elevated with the touch of vodka. Irresistible and my favourite comfort food. This dish is super easy to whip up but tastes restaurant worthy!

Ingredients:

  • 100 ml heavy cream
  • 250g penne pasta
  • 3-5 crushed or finely chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 shallot finely chopped
  • 100 g tomato puree or blended sundried tomatoes
  • Drizzle of olive oil
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • Salt, pepper, chilli flakes, cayenne pepper & paprika to season
  • 1/4 cup parmesan

Optional:

  • 3 tbsp of vodka
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Spinach

Steps:

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with oil and butter.
  2. Add and cook shallots until transparent
  3. Add in garlic and fry until fragrant and golden.
  4. *Stir in tomato. If you're using vodka, stir this in and stir to cook off the alcohol for 3 minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and parmesan cheese until combine.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, chilli, and spices.
  7. Cook pasta according to packet instructions or until al dente. Ensure to save some pasta water for the sauce.
  8. Stir in pasta to the sauce. Et voila! Fin!

*Optional step: Add in the spinach and or mushrooms before the tomatoes.
