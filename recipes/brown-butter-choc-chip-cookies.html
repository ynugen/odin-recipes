Brown Butter Choc-Chip Cookies

Description

The best choc-chip cookies you'll ever taste. Making this will be your new classic. Try out this golden, balanced, and mouth-watering treat.

Ingredients:

  • 168 g unsalted butter
  • 200 g brown sugar, packed
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 220 g all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt + extra for sprinkling
  • 100 g dark chocolate, chopped

Steps:

  1. Brown the butter over medium heat, stirring constantly until the butter foams and becomes a golden brown with a nutty aroma. Take this off the heat and leave to the side to cool down
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combined the cooled brown butter, brown and white sugar. Mix until combined.
  3. Add in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract and mix together.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt, and baking soda and whisk until combined.
  5. Slowly add and mix the dry ingredients into the wet until evenly combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate until just combined. Do not over mix.
  7. Refrigerate the cookie dough for at least an hour.
  8. Preheat the oven to 180ÂºC and line the cookie pan with baking paper. Scoop and roll out cookie dough into even balls, spacing them evenly on the pan.
  9. Bake for 11 min or until golden brown. Let it cool before eating. Et voila! Fin!
