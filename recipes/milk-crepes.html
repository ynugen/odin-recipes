Simple Milk Crêpes

Description:

These simple milk crepes are perfect for an easy, delicious, and elegant breakfast. To elevate this meal, top with fruit, chocolate, maple, or honey!

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 94 g of plain flour
  • 1 tbsp of sugar or sweetener of your choosing
  • 438 ml of full-cream milk or milk alternative of your choosing

Steps:

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs.
  2. Whisk in the flour, sugar and salt and slowly add in the milk until the batter is smooth.
  3. In a pan, melt some butter on medium heat. Scoop in batter until it thinly covers the base.
  4. When the edges are cooked, flip the crepe over. Repeat steps 3-4 for the remaining batter.
  5. Serve the crepes with cream, berries, or sryup. Et voila! Fin!
